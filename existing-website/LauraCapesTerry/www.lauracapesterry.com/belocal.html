Meet The Publisher

Laura Capes Terry, MBA

Laura Capes Terry, MBA, is a StoryBrand Certified Guide, award-winning strategic marketer, and the publisher of BeLocal Georgetown. With over 30 years of marketing experience, she has helped hundreds of local businesses grow by building visibility, credibility, and long-term community relationships.

As both a marketing strategist and a business owner, Laura understands the realities local leaders face in a fast-growing town. BeLocal was created to solve a specific problem: helping trusted businesses get in front of new homeowners at the exact moment they’re forming new buying habits.

Her approach is simple — combine the power of print, the reach of digital, and the trust of community endorsement to create lasting first impressions that turn into lasting relationships.
