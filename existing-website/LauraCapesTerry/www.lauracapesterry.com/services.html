Work With Laura

AI Strategy Sessions

A private, 90-minute strategic session designed to bring clarity to your marketing and confidence to your next decisions—especially in an AI-driven landscape.

Together, we’ll look at what’s working, what’s not, and where AI can actually help—without adding noise, complexity, or losing your voice. This is not about more tools. It’s about better judgment, smarter priorities, and a clear path forward.

Perfect for business owners who feel stretched thin, overwhelmed by AI, or unsure where to focus next—and want thoughtful guidance from someone who understands both strategy and real-world execution.

Fractional Marketing Leadership

Ongoing executive-level marketing leadership for business owners who want clarity, consistency, and momentum—without hiring a full-time CMO.

As your fractional marketing leader, Laura partners with you to clarify your message, guide strategic decisions, integrate AI wisely, and build a marketing approach that supports long-term growth. This is a high-level partnership rooted in trust, accountability, and sound judgment—not task lists or trend-chasing.

Ideal for growing businesses and leadership teams who want a steady guide, strategic oversight, and confident decision-making at the highest level.

Workshops & Speaking

Keynotes and custom trainings focused on building brands with heart in the age of AI.

Laura speaks to entrepreneurs, leadership teams, and organizations about clarity, confidence, trust-based marketing, and navigating AI without losing the human connection that makes businesses thrive. Every session is practical, grounded, and rooted in real-world experience—not hype or theory.

Perfect for faith-filled entrepreneurs, community organizations, and teams who want thoughtful, inspiring guidance that aligns strategy with values.

BeLocal Georgetown

A trusted, multi-media marketing system designed to connect beloved local businesses with new homeowners at the exact moment they’re forming new habits and relationships.

BeLocal functions like a yellow brick road for newcomers—guiding them to the best places to go, the best things to do, and the most trusted local businesses in their new backyard. For businesses, it’s a proactive, relationship-first marketing system that builds awareness, credibility, and long-term loyalty in a growing community.

This is not transactional advertising. It’s a curated, multi-channel ecosystem—print, digital, mobile, and social—designed to help trusted local brands stay visible and relevant as their market evolves.

Perfect for community-minded businesses who want meaningful visibility, long-term relationships, and a presence rooted in trust—not noise.

The Unbridled Creative

A faith-centered video podcast and membership community for entrepreneurs, leaders, and creatives who sense God calling them to build differently—without fear, burnout, or striving.

The Unbridled Creative is where courage meets calling. Through honest conversations, real stories, and practical wisdom, this space helps listeners align their business with their faith, renew their mindset, and lead with peace, clarity, and conviction.

This isn’t about hustle or hype. It’s about inner alignment and outward impact—learning how to think, build, and market from wholeness so your work reflects your values and your calling.

Perfect for faith-filled entrepreneurs and leaders who want to grow with integrity, steward their gifts well, and live unbridled in both life and business.

Be Locally Loved

A limited-edition video podcast capturing the real, often unseen impact of BeLocal in communities across the country.

Be Locally Loved exists to preserve and share the true story of BeLocal—not as a magazine, but as a proactive, community-building system that welcomes new residents, strengthens local businesses, supports cities, and shapes the fabric of thriving communities. Through a curated series of conversations with the top BeLocal Area Directors across the country, the series documents how trust is built, relationships are formed, and impact compounds over time.

This is not a sales platform. It’s a storytelling archive—designed to clarify BeLocal’s long-term value, elevate its mission, and ensure its impact is clearly understood by business owners, civic leaders, and future publishers.

Perfect for community leaders, city partners, business owners, and fellow N2 Area Directors who want to understand how BeLocal works and the impact it makes on the communities we serve.
