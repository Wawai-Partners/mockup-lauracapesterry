**Websites: The Key to Turning Curiosity into Action** 

*A Guide for Local Business Owners by Laura Capes Terry*, *MBA* 

When people see your business in BeLocal, they want to learn more. The first place they go is your website. Here's the problem: most visitors decide in just a few seconds whether to stay or leave. If your homepage doesn't clearly communicate what you do, who it's for, and what to do next without scrolling, you could be losing them before they ever become a customer. 

This checklist will help you make sure the most important part of your website\-the above\-the\-fold section \-grabs attention, builds trust, and invites action so you can turn curiosity into new business. 

**Clear, benefit\-driven headline** 

• One sentence that explains what you do and who you help. 

**Supporting subhead** 

• Adds context or highlights the transformation/result you provide. 

**One strong call to action (CTA) button** 

·   
Make it visually obvious and action\-oriented (e.g., "Book a Call," "Download the Guide"). 

**High-quality visual or image** 

• Should reflect your audience, your work, or the success they're seeking. 

**Early trust signal** 

• A testimonial, a "5 stars" badge, or "Trusted by" logos can go a long way. 

**Simple navigation** 

•   
Stick to 3-5 key links. Don't overwhelm them with options. 

**Avoid distractions** 

• No sliders, autoplay videos, or too many competing elements. 

**Brand voice is clear and confident** 

• Speak directly to your ideal customer's needs and goals. 

**Mobile-friendly design** 

• Ensure your site loads quickly, looks great, and functions seamlessly on smartphones and tablets. 

Pro Tip: Ask someone who doesn't know your business to visit your site for 10 seconds and answer: 

\>\>\> **What do I offer? Who is it for? What should I do next?** 

If you're unsure whether your homepage is doing its job, revisit this checklist and make updates until it's clear, compelling, and action\-oriented. Remember, you don't have long to make a great first impression\-but when you get it right, you open the door to more leads, more customers, and more growth.
