## **BELOCAL GEORGETOWN**

**BeLocal Georgetown is Laura's award-winning local media company — a trusted resource that Georgetown residents and newcomers turn to for discovering the best local businesses.**

Being featured in BeLocal means your business is introduced to a highly engaged local audience that's actively looking for trusted recommendations — not just scrolling past ads.

### **Print**

BeLocal Georgetown's beautifully designed print magazine is distributed to new and established residents throughout Georgetown. Your business is presented in a trusted, curated format that readers actually save and reference.

### **Digital**

Your business listing and story reaches BeLocal's online audience through the website, email newsletters, and digital content — extending your print presence into the digital world.

### **Mobile**

Georgetown residents access BeLocal on their phones every day. Mobile-optimized content ensures your business is easy to find, share, and act on — wherever your customers are.

### **Social**

BeLocal amplifies sponsor stories across social media platforms, putting your business in front of followers who trust BeLocal's recommendations and actively engage with local content.

### **Events**

BeLocal Georgetown hosts and participates in local events that bring business owners and community members face-to-face. Event sponsorship puts your business in front of a live, engaged audience.

## **LET'S GO LOCALS DIRECTORY & STORIES**

Let's Go Locals is a curated directory of Georgetown's best local businesses — designed to help residents and visitors discover, support, and recommend local over national chains.

Being listed in Let's Go Locals places your business in a community-first platform that celebrates what makes Georgetown unique.
